Benchmark Overview

C2CAD-Bench evaluates the multi-step spatial reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) in generating deterministic CAD geometry. The benchmark requires models to map high-level engineering specifications to precise parametric primitives. Unlike existing benchmarks that focus on simple shape production, C2CAD-Bench emphasizes topological accuracy, parametric derivation, and structural constraints.

75+ Cases

25 distinct test families across three complexity levels, from basic primitives to complex bio-inspired assemblies.

13 Frontier LLMs

Comparative evaluation of state-of-the-art models including Gemini 3.1, GPT-4, Claude 3.5, and DeepSeek R1.

Tri-Metric Scoring

Automated pipeline covering Coverage (20%), Geometry (30%), and Semantic Validation (50%) against ground truth.

Metric Decomposition

Our scoring methodology prioritizes structural and functional validity over mere shape production. We decompose the evaluation into three primary dimensions:

  • Coverage: Ensuring the correct number and type of geometric primitives are generated.
  • Geometry: Precise positional and dimensional accuracy against parametric ground truth.
  • Semantic: Validating physics (gravity, connectivity) and family-specific engineering constraints.
